``` name: Snakes on a train company: Global Game Jam timeframe: Jan 2012 techUsed: ['Unity3d','C#'] funProjectOrder: 1 ```

During the brainstorm session, someone jokingly pitched a parody of Snakes on a plane, taking part on a subway train in new york, filled with snake, and the only person that can save the day is Samuel Mongoose Jackson. We decided to just take that funny idea and roll with it.
